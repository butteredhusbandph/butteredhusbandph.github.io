I had a spare `Raspberry Pi 4` sitting around, so I decided to repurpose it as a [Pihole](https://pi-hole.net/). My router already supports DNS forwarding to services like AdGuard and ControlD, but I wanted a setup that blocks unwanted DNS queries before they even leave my network.
